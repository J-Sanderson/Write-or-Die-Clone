JSanderson's Write or Die Clone

A clone of the old webapp version of DrWicked's Write Or Die, for those who liked it. Write things, or there will be consequences.

Pick your settings:


Gentle - write or you will receive a reminder to keep writing.
Intermediate - write or annoying sounds will play.
Kamikaze - write or your work will delete itself!






WARNING: this page does not save your words. If you want to keep them, copy-paste them somewhere safe when you're done.

Get writing!
